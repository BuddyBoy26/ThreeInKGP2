My Three Favourite Places in IIT Khargapur

Main Building

No no, not the clichéd main building. I am talking about the library's exit, early morning, at 5 am. It is truly a sight to behold. I don't grab a photo of it, but imagine a cold sunrrise while leaving the library with clouds and birds chirping.
Feel aa gaya yaar!

MMM, DSK, E416

Okay, it stays messy most of the time, but its the place only where you can do whatever you want, however you want, without restrictions. Especially with amazing friends and a chilled out room mate. It may not be the best place in KGP, but it is where you can be yourself, no questions asked.
Sleeping at 5am or waking up at 5 am, its your call. Yes, but its upto you what you do there. It's what I'd call the make or break spot.

Nescafé

Nescafé at any hall is love (maybe not LBS). Close to your comfort zone, anything you want, its pretty cool. Maggi at midnight or mayber even chaat, you got it. The number of times we've midsnacked is maddening!
